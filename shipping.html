Shipping Policy

We primarily serve customers in store at Lawrence Road, Amritsar — jewellery is best chosen in person. For confirmed orders that need delivery, the following applies.

Delivery

  • Ready pieces can usually be delivered within Amritsar within 2–4 working days after payment confirmation.
  • Outstation delivery within India is arranged through fully insured, reputed couriers; timelines are typically 7–14 working days.
  • Made-to-order and customized pieces take 15–30 days to craft before dispatch.

Insurance & verification

  • Every shipment is insured in transit. Packages are sealed and may require identity verification on delivery.
  • Please verify the seal before accepting the package and report any tampering immediately.

Charges

Shipping and insurance charges, where applicable, are confirmed with you on WhatsApp or phone before dispatch.
